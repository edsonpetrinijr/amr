# Ideas Backlog

> Where raw ideas go to be matured, validated, and either promoted, parked, or killed.
> Maintained by the **Idea Strategist** agent. Newest at top.
>
> Each idea moves through stages: **raw → exploring → validated → promoted** (or **parked** / **killed**).
>
> Entry format:
> ```
> ## <idea name>
> - **Stage:** raw | exploring | validated | promoted | parked | killed
> - **Captured:** YYYY-MM-DD
> - **The idea:** one or two sentences
> - **Why it might matter:** the bet / the upside
> - **Biggest risk / unknown:** the thing that would kill it
> - **Validation so far:** what's been checked, by whom (which agent), what was found
> - **Verdict / next step:** promote to a spec, park, kill, or what to test next
> - **Experiment branch:** idea/<slug> (if a prototype was spun up)
> ```
